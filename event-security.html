Our Capabilities

What We Provide in Event Security

From intimate private gatherings to multi-thousand-person festivals, J&B Security Solutions scales its approach to fit your event's exact needs.

Guest Screening & Access Control

Bag checks, credential verification, and wristband enforcement at all entry points to prevent unauthorized access and maintain a safe guest experience.

Crowd Management & Flow Control

Strategically positioned officers managing guest movement, line control, and capacity limits to prevent dangerous overcrowding situations.

VIP & Talent Protection

Dedicated close protection for performers, keynote speakers, and high-profile guests — discreet, professional, and trained in executive protection techniques.

Venue Perimeter Security

Monitoring and securing event perimeters, backstage access points, vendor areas, and green rooms to prevent unauthorized entry at all positions.

Incident Response Protocols

All J&B event officers are trained in de-escalation, first-response protocols, and coordination with local law enforcement for seamless handling of any incident.

Post-Event Overnight Security

Coverage after your event closes until venue staff arrive the following morning — protecting equipment, merchandise, and the venue from theft or vandalism.
