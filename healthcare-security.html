Our Capabilities

What We Provide in Healthcare Security

Every J&B officer deployed to a healthcare environment receives facility-specific training and understands the unique demands of protecting patients, staff, and visitors.

Patient & Visitor Access Control

Managing lobby entry, restricting unauthorized access to patient floors, and enforcing visitor policies while maintaining a welcoming, non-intimidating presence.

Behavioral Health De-escalation

Officers trained in verbal de-escalation techniques for psychiatric and behavioral health units, reducing use-of-force incidents and protecting both patients and clinical staff.

Hospital Lobby & ER Security

Stationing security personnel at emergency department entry points to screen individuals, diffuse high-tension situations, and support nursing staff during difficult interactions.

Medical Office Building Protection

Security for multi-tenant MOBs including parking garage patrols, after-hours access management, and weekend coverage when administrative staff are absent.

HIPAA-Aware Security Protocols

All officers are briefed on HIPAA compliance as it relates to security responsibilities — protecting patient privacy while carrying out incident reporting and documentation duties.

24/7 Overnight & Weekend Coverage

Continuous staffing for evening, overnight, and holiday shifts — ensuring full coverage when your facility is most vulnerable and administrative support is limited.
