Credentials & Compliance

Licensed, Insured & Background-Checked

Florida Class D License (Unarmed)

All unarmed officers hold a valid Class D Security Officer license issued by the Florida Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services (FDACS).

Florida Class G License (Armed)

Armed officers carry valid Florida Class G Statewide Firearm Licenses and meet all training requirements mandated by Florida Statute Chapter 493.

Comprehensive Background Checks

Every officer undergoes state and federal criminal background screening, drug testing, and reference verification before being deployed to any client site.

Fully Insured Operations

J&B Security Solutions carries comprehensive general liability and professional liability insurance, providing full protection for our clients on every engagement.
