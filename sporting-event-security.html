Our Capabilities

What We Provide in Sporting Event Security

From youth leagues in local parks to professional venues, J&B delivers a complete security operation for every level of sporting event.

Gate & Ticket Check-In Control

Managing entry lanes, ticket verification, prohibited items enforcement, and credentialing at all stadium and venue entry points to ensure a smooth, secure arrival experience.

Field, Court & Bench Area Access

Protecting restricted areas on the field, court, and bench — preventing fan encroachment, media overstep, and unauthorized athlete access during competition.

Parking Lot Arrival & Departure Management

Officers stationed in parking areas managing vehicle flow, pedestrian crossings, tailgate areas, and post-game departure to minimize congestion and prevent altercations.

Roving Security Officers

Mobile officers patrolling concourse areas, concession zones, restrooms, and stands — providing visible deterrence and rapid response throughout the entire venue footprint.

Fan Conduct & Crowd Safety

De-escalating disputes, managing intoxicated fans, and enforcing venue codes of conduct in a manner that preserves the game-day atmosphere without unnecessary confrontation.

Coordination with Venue Staff

Seamless integration with your venue operations team, law enforcement liaisons, and medical personnel — ensuring a unified command structure for the entire event.
